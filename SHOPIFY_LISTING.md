## Full Description

### The Problem

Retail staffing is still mostly guesswork. Managers look at last week's schedule, make a rough estimate, and hope it holds up. When a busy Saturday hits and you're two team members short — or a slow Tuesday drains your labour budget — you've already lost.

### The Solution

MAinager connects directly to your Shopify store and turns your actual order history into actionable staffing recommendations. It shows you when your busiest hours and days really are, then helps you build a schedule that matches demand — so you're staffed right, every shift.

### Key Benefits

- **Data-driven scheduling** — Staffing recommendations are powered by your real Shopify sales trends, not gut instinct.
- **Complete workforce hub** — Scheduling, time tracking, payroll reporting, team communication, and task management all in one place.
- **AI-assisted management** — Built-in AI copilot (MAinager AI) answers operational questions, drafts communications, and surfaces insights from your store data.
- **Clock-in with confidence** — GPS-verified clock-in and clock-out, with automatic timesheet generation ready for payroll export.
- **Keep your team in the loop** — Team messaging, shift notifications, and a daily Morning Huddle briefing keep every shift running smoothly.
- **Lightweight, minimal permissions** — MAinager only requests `read_orders` and `read_products` — we never touch your customer data, payments, or store settings.

### How It Works

1. Install MAinager and connect your Shopify store with one click.
2. MAinager syncs your historical order data and identifies your busiest periods.
3. Build your weekly schedule using the visual drag-and-drop scheduler informed by those trends.
4. Your team receives shift notifications, can clock in/out from their phones, and communicate through the built-in messaging system.
5. At the end of each pay period, export timesheets directly to your payroll provider.
