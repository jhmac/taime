# MAinager — Feature List for GTM Strategy

**Product:** MAinager — The Boutique Operating System
**Target Market:** Independent retail boutiques (1–5 locations, 3–15 employees)
**Tagline:** One app to run your store. Scheduling, SOPs, tasks, and AI — built for boutique teams.

---

## Core Feature Categories

### 1. Smart Time Clock & Attendance
Employees clock in and out from their phone with location verification. The system uses geofencing to confirm employees are on-site, tracks breaks for compliance, flags overtime, and handles auto clock-out if someone forgets. Managers see who's on shift in real time.

### 2. AI-Powered Scheduling
Generate optimized weekly schedules in one click. The AI considers employee availability, historical sales data from Shopify, minimum staffing requirements, and store hours. Managers can adjust shifts on a drag-and-drop grid, and employees can submit availability and request time off directly in the app.

### 3. Standard Operating Procedures (SOP Library)
A digital library of every store procedure — opening routines, closing checklists, inventory counts, customer service protocols. Owners build SOPs with step-by-step instructions (including photos and timers), and employees run them as interactive checklists on their phone. Includes a Training Hub for onboarding new hires automatically.

### 4. SOP Intelligence & Evolution
AI continuously analyzes how SOPs are being executed — which steps take too long, which get skipped, where new employees struggle. It surfaces actionable insights for managers and even proposes specific revisions to improve procedures over time.

### 5. Decision Tree SOPs
For complex procedures with branching logic (e.g., "Is the customer returning an online order or in-store purchase?"), owners can build visual decision trees that guide employees through the right path based on the situation.

### 6. Task Management
Assign, track, and complete daily tasks. Managers create tasks with due dates and priority levels. Employees see their to-do list on their dashboard. Overdue tasks are flagged automatically.

### 7. GTD Workflow Engine (Getting Things Done)
A personal productivity system built into the app. Employees and managers can capture ideas, to-dos, and observations into an inbox. AI classifies each item — is it a quick task, a project, or something to defer? Includes a guided weekly review ritual.

### 8. Smart Task Suggestions (AI Review Assistant)
Every day, the AI answers "What should I be doing right now?" for each employee. It considers their assigned tasks, upcoming SOPs, open issues, GTD actions, and current schedule to generate a prioritized action list with urgency levels and time estimates.

### 9. Ask MAinager (AI Copilot)
A conversational AI assistant available from any screen. Employees can ask questions like "How do I process a return?", "Who's working tomorrow?", or "What's our busiest hour?" The AI draws on store data, SOPs, schedules, and sales to give accurate, actionable answers.

### 10. Morning Whisper (Owner/Manager Briefing)
An AI-generated daily briefing delivered to owners and managers each morning. Summarizes yesterday's performance, highlights anything unusual, and previews today's priorities — like a personal chief of staff.

### 11. Daily Rituals System
Structured daily touchpoints that build team culture:
- **Morning Huddle** — AI-generated agenda with wins, goals, and a lean principle of the day
- **Midday Pulse** — A quick team check-in surfaced mid-shift
- **Daily Debrief** — End-of-day reflection capturing what went well and what bugged you
- **Kudos** — Peer recognition moments
- **Improvement Quote** — A daily motivational message

### 12. Issue Tracker
Employees report problems (broken equipment, process gaps, safety concerns) directly from their phone. Managers track, prioritize, and resolve issues with full history. AI monitors patterns across issues to surface systemic problems.

### 13. Kudos Wall
A peer-to-peer recognition feed where team members publicly appreciate each other. Builds morale and makes good work visible to the whole team.

### 14. Improvement Video Platform
Employees record and share 60-second improvement videos — quick wins, better ways to do something, or lessons learned. Creates a living library of team knowledge.

### 15. Lean Board
A team-level dashboard tracking improvement metrics over time — how many improvements were submitted, SOP compliance rates, issue resolution speed, and participation. Includes trend charts and weekly AI-generated summaries.

### 16. In-App Messaging
Real-time team communication with direct messages, group chats, and channels. Threaded conversations keep discussions organized. No need for a separate messaging app.

### 17. AI Background Insights Engine
The AI continuously analyzes store data behind the scenes — scheduling patterns, time clock anomalies, task completion trends, SOP compliance — and surfaces proactive recommendations on the manager and owner dashboards. No manual report-pulling required.

### 18. RAG-Powered SOP Search
Employees can search SOPs using natural language (e.g., "how to handle a gift card refund"). The system uses semantic search to find relevant procedures even when the exact words don't match.

### 19. Role-Based Dashboards
Three distinct dashboard experiences:
- **Associates** see their tasks, schedule, and personal stats
- **Managers** see team status, open issues, daily rituals, and operational metrics
- **Owners** see business health, sales data, labor costs, and strategic insights

### 20. Geofencing & Location Security
Define store boundaries on a map. The system enforces that employees can only clock in when physically at the store. Supports circular and polygon boundaries, configurable grace periods, and live location monitoring.

### 21. Payroll Management
Track hours, calculate pay with overtime and holiday multipliers, and manage pay periods. Supports both W-2 employees and 1099 contractors. Includes AI-powered holiday pay rules that understand natural language (e.g., "time and a half on federal holidays").

### 22. Shopify Integration
Connects to your Shopify POS to pull in sales data. This powers the AI scheduling (staffing based on sales patterns), owner dashboard revenue snapshots, and Morning Whisper briefings.

### 23. Weekly Review Ritual
A guided end-of-week reflection powered by AI. Reviews what got done, what's still open, and what to focus on next week. Helps managers and owners stay on top of operations without endless spreadsheets.

### 24. Progressive Web App (PWA) with Offline Support
Works on any phone or tablet — no app store download required. Employees add it to their home screen and it works like a native app, including offline mode for when internet is spotty. Data syncs automatically when connectivity returns.

### 25. Employee Onboarding Automation
When a new hire is added, the system automatically surfaces required training SOPs, assigns onboarding tasks, and tracks their progress through the Training Hub. Managers can see at a glance who's completed what.
